Playoff Porta Potties

The class has a little fun with icons.

Concourse Village in the Bronx has seen an uptick in quality of life issues including public urination.
The uptick is believed to be a consequence of lowering the penalty from a summons to a fine. The change was made as a response to activists' concerns that a disproportionate amount of minorities were being prosecuted for minor offenses.
One project addressing this issue provides public porta potties on a heavily trafficked thoroughfare.
The project, sponsored by the 161st Street B.I.D., began at the start of the New York Yankees' postseason.
